# Research — retention, motivation & reward design

Background research that informs Cetele's product & UX decisions. Not status (see
`.claude/STATUS.md`) and not the spec (see `docs/PRD.md`) — this is the _evidence
base_ behind why we build the retention mechanics the way we do.

| Doc                                                                        | What it covers                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       |
| -------------------------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| [`01-competitive-landscape.md`](01-competitive-landscape.md)               | Apps that win at daily reminders + progress tracking (Duolingo, Habitica, Finch, Forest, Streaks, + Muslim Salah apps), and a **gap analysis** of what they have that Cetele doesn't.                                                                                |
| [`02-motivation-and-reward-design.md`](02-motivation-and-reward-design.md) | The psychology of returning daily (dopamine, loss aversion, goal-gradient, SDT, Fogg, Hook, Octalysis), Cetele's **reward ladder**, the **right level of abstraction** (what to show / hide), ethics guardrails, and a prioritised backlog.                          |
| [`03-feature-recommendations.md`](03-feature-recommendations.md)           | **The synthesis & decision doc** — links the psychology (02) to the competitive gaps (01) into a single opinionated, prioritised answer to _what to build_. Includes the verdict on the "accountability pet" (→ a collective **group garden**) and a phased roadmap. |
